Get to Know Me

My Digital Perspective

I’m Tamjeed Chowdhury — a multidisciplinary freelancer with a background in psychology, a creative eye for design, and a passion for storytelling through both visuals and sound. I work at the intersection of art, technology, and psychology, combining my experience in data analysis, behavioral science, and creative production to bring meaningful ideas to life.

Whether I’m sketching concepts, animating full scenes, or producing music inspired by artists like A$AP Rocky, or Indie games like Celeste and Hollow Knight, my work is grounded in one core belief: freedom for the individual is essential. I use my creativity to challenge norms, build emotion, and reflect the world as it is — and what it could be.

Right now, I’m focused on gaining experience in the healthcare industry while building a creative portfolio that showcases the full range of my work, from digital sketches and animation to music production. I consider myself a jack of all trades with a mission: to uplift, connect, and improve human lives.
